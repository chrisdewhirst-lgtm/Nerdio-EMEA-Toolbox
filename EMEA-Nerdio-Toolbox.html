How This Works

1
Green "Ready to Use" tools work instantly — just open the HTML file in your browser. No data needed.
2
Orange "Build Template" files are instructions for Claude. Upload the MD file + your data, and Claude builds your personalised version.
3
Blue "Reference" files are EUC ecosystem knowledge — maps, architectures, vendor landscapes for customer conversations.
4
Purple ".skill" files install into Claude Desktop/Cowork to give you an always-on Nerdio AI assistant.
