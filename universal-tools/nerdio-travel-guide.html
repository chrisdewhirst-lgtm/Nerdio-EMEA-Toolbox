How we get you there safely

No surprise delays. No lost luggage. No 5am check-ins. Here's the typical journey from where you are to your destination — with Nerdio as your personal travel companion at every stop.

Pre-Departure — Discovery
The Route Survey: Understanding Your Environment
Before any great journey, you need to know what you're working with. We map your existing estate — licences, workloads, user profiles, Azure consumption, and any hidden infrastructure skeletons in the data centre wardrobe.
Nerdio does this: Free environment assessment with our pre-sales team. We size your Azure deployment with the Nerdio Cost Estimator — pinpoint accuracy before you spend a penny.
🎯
Pre-Departure — Proof of Value
The Test Drive: Proof of Concept or Proof of Value
You wouldn't book a two-week holiday at a hotel you've never seen a photo of. We run a structured PoV — typically 30 days — where you experience the Nerdio platform against your real workloads in your real Azure environment.

Departure — Migration
Wheels Up: The Migration Flight
This is where the journey truly begins. Phased migration from your legacy environment — Citrix, VMware, RDSH, or native Azure — into a Nerdio-managed modern cloud desktop estate. Workloads migrate in waves, users barely notice.
Nerdio does this: Nerdio Migration Services (NMS) — a dedicated professional services offer with migration tooling, runbooks, hypercare support, and change management guidance. Not outsourced. Nerdio-led.
🏨
Arrival — Go-Live
Checking In: First 90 Days
You're here. The pool is open. But the first 90 days are critical — this is when cost optimisation kicks in, auto-scaling starts saving money, and your IT team discovers they have a Friday afternoon again.
Nerdio does this: Go-Live Engineering support. Your Customer Success Manager is assigned on day one. Auto-scaling activated. Rightsizing recommendations surfaced. Azure cost reporting baseline established.
🌅
In-Resort — Steady State
Living the Dream: Ongoing Operations
This isn't a one-week break — it's where you live now. Nerdio keeps optimising your environment continuously: auto-scaling Azure compute up and down by the hour, flagging idle resources, enforcing governance policies, and keeping your security posture current.
Nerdio does this: Always-on automation. Patented auto-scaling. Scripted Actions library. Azure cost dashboards. Policy enforcement. Helpdesk delegation. Everything, always on.
🚀
Future-Proofing — What's Next
Extending the Holiday: AI Agents & Beyond
The cloud desktop isn't the end destination — it's the base camp. From here, your organisation can run CUA/AI agents on AVD infrastructure, expand into AVD on Azure Local for sovereign workloads, and manage the AI era with the same Nerdio control plane.
Nerdio does this: AVD as the control plane for AI agent workloads. Azure Local + Azure Arc management. Nerdio Nucleus. The only platform spanning cloud, sovereign, and hybrid — from a single console.
