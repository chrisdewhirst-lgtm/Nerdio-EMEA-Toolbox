# Nerdio Nucleus — Cowork Setup Guide

> **How to use this:** Upload this document into Cowork as your AI instruction set. Then work through the setup sections below — each one tells you what to type in AND what files to upload alongside it. Nucleus adapts to your function (Sales, Marketing, CS, Partner Enablement, or all of the above), so the more context you give it, the better it performs. Think of this as onboarding a new colleague who needs to understand you, your role, your accounts, and your company before they can truly help.

---

## QUICK START — DO THESE FOUR THINGS FIRST

Before anything else, upload these if you have them — they give Nucleus immediate context:

**Upload now:**
- [ ] Your CV or LinkedIn profile export (PDF or Word)
- [ ] Any recent piece of work you're proud of (email, proposal, deck, campaign, QBR — whatever represents your best output)
- [ ] The Nerdio brand guide (PDF — available from the Nerdio partner portal)

This confirms Nucleus has absorbed your context correctly before you start using it.

This is critical. Nucleus will mirror your voice in every email, proposal, blog post, and message it drafts.

**Type this in:**


**Upload to support this section:**

## SETUP COMPLETE — HOW TO USE NUCLEUS

Once you've worked through the sections above, Nucleus is ready. Here's what to ask by function:

### Sales
- *"Write a cold outreach email to the CISO at [COMPANY] — they're a Citrix customer considering cloud migration."*
- *"Build me a discovery question set for my call with [ACCOUNT] tomorrow."*
- *"Create an account plan for [ACCOUNT] based on what I've uploaded."*
- *"The customer just said [OBJECTION]. How do I respond?"*
- *"Help me build the business case for [ACCOUNT]."*

### Marketing
- *"Write a blog post about the hidden cost of managing AVD natively — aim for 800 words, insight-led."*
- *"Draft a campaign brief for a Citrix migration webinar targeting enterprise CIOs."*
- *"Create a LinkedIn post series (3 posts) around our latest case study."*
- *"Build me an analyst briefing document for [ANALYST FIRM]."*
- *"Write the email nurture sequence for our W365 whitepaper download."*

### Customer Success
- *"Prepare my QBR deck for [CUSTOMER] — their renewal is in 60 days."*
- *"[CUSTOMER] has low feature adoption after 90 days. Draft an enablement re-engagement plan."*
- *"Build a value realization report for [CUSTOMER] showing their savings since deployment."*
- *"Draft a renewal business case for [CUSTOMER]'s CFO."*
- *"What expansion opportunities should I pursue with [CUSTOMER]?"*

### Partner Enablement
- *"Create a partner pitch deck for [PARTNER] to use with their mid-market prospects."*
- *"Build an enablement guide for [PARTNER]'s sales team — they're new to selling Nerdio."*
- *"Draft a co-sell proposal for [PARTNER]'s Microsoft ATU contact."*
- *"What does [PARTNER] need to do to reach Gold tier?"*

### Microsoft Alignment
- *"How do I position Nerdio to my Microsoft ATU contact as a co-sell opportunity?"*
- *"Write a Microsoft-facing one-pager showing how Nerdio accelerates W365 adoption."*

### Internal Knowledge
- *"What's the difference between NME Standard and Premium?"*
- *"How does Nerdio handle auto-scaling technically?"*
- *"What's our competitive positioning against Omnissa?"*
- *"Give me the latest platform stats for a presentation."*
