🔒 MUST READ

Keep customer data safe.
Use the Pseudonym Manager.

Before you paste anything into Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI tool — pseudonymise it first. The Pseudonym Manager does it in seconds.
STEP 01
Map Your Data
Auto-generate pseudonyms for companies, contacts, phone numbers, and deal values.
how it mapsCompanies get Greek alphabet names, contacts get realistic fake names, phones become 555-numbers, deal values become obfuscated ranges. All auto-generated.
STEP 02
One-Click Swap
Paste your text, click pseudonymise, all mapped terms are replaced. Copy the clean version.
example"Schroders plc" becomes "Alpha Financial Corp". "James Moon" becomes "Robert Chen". The AI never sees real data.
STEP 03
Work in AI Safely
Use Claude or any AI tool with pseudonymised data. No customer names, no compliance risk.
the key ruleAI tools see only pseudonyms. They can still do analysis, write emails, build proposals — they just don't know who the real customer is.
STEP 04
Reverse Swap Back
Take the AI output and reverse-swap in approved internal tools only. Real data never touches external AI.
importantDe-pseudonymisation should only happen in approved internal systems like Copilot or your local machine. Never paste real data back into external AI.
